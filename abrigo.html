Abrigo

Problem: 'Explore technology and make something to exhibit at an event, the theme is "Distorional Evolution".'

Insight: The theme doesn't mean anything, we can build the most beautiful thing we can dream up.

Solution: An Interactive art installation where the user can control the music, sounds, visuals and environment. We combined a Backstory and props together with a Kinect camera connected to Processing, Synapse and Ableton to create an immersive art experience: Abrigo.
